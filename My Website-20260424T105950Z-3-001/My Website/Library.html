Library / E-Library

Fazaia Bilquis College of Education for Women (FBCEW) features a well-stocked physical library and a dedicated digital library section.

Physical Library
The college library provides a supportive learning environment with access to a wide range of materials and resources.
Collection: The library holds the latest books on science, mathematics, education, training, language, and literature, along with general reference books.
Periodicals: The subscription list includes popular magazines like Newsweek, Time, Reader's Digest, and Forum, as well as newspapers such as the Daily Jang, Nawa-e-Waqt, and The News.
Facilities: The library is equipped with sufficient computers and internet access, and includes several study areas.

E-Library (Digital Library)
The digital library section enhances student research capabilities by providing access to electronic resources.
Equipment: It features a dedicated section with 8 computers and a high-speed internet connection.
Resources: Students can access and download reference books and articles, suggested by their teachers, from paid websites and digital resources.
Affiliation Benefits: As an affiliate institute of Air University, students and faculty may have access to the extensive HEC National Digital Library Program, which includes thousands of e-books and electronic journals, typically available within the university premises or via VPN for eligible faculty and scholars.
