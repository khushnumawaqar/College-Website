Welcome to Fazaia Bilquis College
of Education for Women
PAF Base Nur Khan

Fazaia Bilquis College of Education for Women, PAF Base Nur Khan is a women’s teacher-education and degree college under the Pakistan Air Force Women’s Association (PAFWA). It was established in 1984, originally as Mujahida Academy, and later developed into a full-fledged college focused on women’s education.

The college is affiliated with Air University, Islamabad, and is known for its quality teaching, research, and community contribution.
